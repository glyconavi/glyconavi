Definition

Glycan

The terms glycan is defined as synonyms meaning "compounds consisting of a large number of monosaccharides linked glycosidically". In addition the term glycan may also be used to refer to the carbohydrate component of a glycoconjugate, such as a glycoprotein, glycolipid, or a proteoglycan.
